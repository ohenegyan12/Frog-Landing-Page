Frequently Asked Questions and Guides

Below we have provided frequently asked questions about FROG, its integration and a few others. If you have any other questions, please get in touch by talking to an expert!

How do I send personalized messages?

+

You can personalize messages using custom fields in your contact list. This allows you to include individual recipient details like names, account numbers, or any other custom information in your messages.

How to send a simple message?

+

To send a simple message:

  1. Log into your FROG account
  2. Click on "New Message"
  3. Enter the recipient's number or select from contacts
  4. Type your message
  5. Click "Send"

How to send bulk message to contacts from a file?

+

To send bulk messages from a file:

  1. Prepare your contact file (Excel/CSV)
  2. Go to the Bulk Message section
  3. Upload your contact file
  4. Map the columns to the required fields
  5. Compose your message
  6. Review and send

How do I send bulk message to groups?

+

To send messages to groups:

  1. Go to the Groups section
  2. Select the target group(s)
  3. Click "Send Message"
  4. Compose your message
  5. Review and send

How to send a simple email message?

+

To send a simple email:

  1. Navigate to the Email section
  2. Click "New Email"
  3. Enter recipient email address
  4. Compose your email
  5. Send

Steps on how to send bulk email message to groups

+

For bulk email to groups:

  1. Select the Email Groups section
  2. Choose your target group(s)
  3. Click "Compose Email"
  4. Create your email content
  5. Review and send

How to send bulk email message to contacts from a file

+

To send bulk emails using a file:

  1. Prepare your email list file
  2. Go to Bulk Email section
  3. Upload your file
  4. Map the email column
  5. Compose your email
  6. Send

Steps on how to upload contacts into a group

+

To upload contacts to a group:

  1. Go to Groups section
  2. Select or create a group
  3. Click "Import Contacts"
  4. Upload your contact file
  5. Map the columns
  6. Complete the import

How to delete Groups?

+

To delete groups:

  1. Navigate to Groups section
  2. Select the group(s) to delete
  3. Click the delete icon
  4. Confirm deletion

How to group unassigned contacts?

+

To group unassigned contacts:

  1. Go to Contacts section
  2. Filter for unassigned contacts
  3. Select contacts to group
  4. Click "Add to Group"
  5. Choose or create a group
  6. Confirm the assignment

How to Add Funds?

+

To add funds to your account:

  1. Click on "Add Funds" or "Buy Credits"
  2. Select your preferred payment method
  3. Enter the amount you wish to add
  4. Complete the payment process
  5. Credits will be added automatically
