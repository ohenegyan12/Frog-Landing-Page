Frequently Asked Questions

What are SMS credits?

SMS credits are the number of text messages you can send each month. One credit equals one standard SMS message (160 characters). Longer messages may use multiple credits.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes, you can change your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll be prorated for the remainder of the billing cycle. When downgrading, changes take effect at the start of the next billing cycle.

Do unused credits roll over?

No, SMS credits reset at the beginning of each billing cycle. However, you can purchase additional credits if you need more during your current cycle.

What kind of support do you offer?

We offer email support for all plans, with priority support and dedicated account managers for Professional and Enterprise plans. Enterprise customers also receive 24/7 support coverage.
